About TEDxSVECW

It all started with a spark—a vision to open new doors for our college and connect it to a world of ideas and inspiration. My co-organizer and I dreamed of creating a culture that thrived on learning, collaboration, and bold conversations. It began with simple plans: inviting guest speakers, hosting webinars, and fostering meaningful exchanges. But we wanted more than just moments of inspiration; we wanted a movement. That’s when TEDx entered the picture. The idea of bringing a global platform like TEDx to our campus felt like the perfect way to amplify voices, ignite creativity, and build a legacy of innovation. TEDxSVECW became our mission—a stage for ideas worth spreading and stories worth hearing. This year, we dive deeper than ever with the theme “Sea of Voices.” Like the ocean’s infinite waves, this theme embodies the boundless diversity of thoughts and perspectives. Each voice is a wave, carrying its unique story to the shore and adding richness to the greater conversation. Through this journey, we aim to uncover hidden insights, challenge conventions, and celebrate the profound power of shared ideas. Welcome to TEDxSVECW—a voyage through a Sea of Voices, where every wave leaves an indelible mark.

Speakers

Anurag Mittal
Mr. Anurag Mittal is the CEO and Co-founder of Olao Books, a startup aiming to transform accounting and payroll software for small and medium-sized businesses. With leadership roles at Deloitte, JP Morgan, and Icertis, he brings a unique mix of sales, marketing, and operational expertise. Anurag's journey from marketing leader to finance and accounting entrepreneur showcases his versatility and vision. He is dedicated to changing how businesses handle their financial processes, ensuring they have the tools and insights to thrive in a competitive world.
Pooja Vegnesa
Ms. Pooja Vegesna is a dedicated mental health counselor passionate about promoting slow living and mindfulness. As a social influencer, she shares insights on mental wellness and self-care, encouraging her followers to embrace a more intentional lifestyle. An avid yoga practitioner, Pooja integrates the principles of yoga into her counseling approach, helping individuals find balance and peace. She enjoys reading and sharing knowledge, striving to empower others on their journeys to mental well-being.
Anuvamshi Shetty
Ms. Anuvamshi Shetty began her career in investment banking, gaining valuable insights into finance's intricacies. However, a pivotal realization soon reshaped her goals, and many talented women faced significant obstacles in achieving financial independence. This awareness inspired her to pursue a higher purpose, empowering women nationwide to build businesses, gain financial freedom, and overcome societal barriers. Today, she leads a platform dedicated to providing guidance and financial support to female entrepreneurs, helping them forge their paths to success. Anuvamshi’s efforts have garnered recognition, including acknowledgment from MIT Boston’s Deshpande Startups, underscoring her belief that even the smallest actions can spark meaningful change. Beyond her entrepreneurial ventures, she embraces a creative and multifaceted life, exploring roles as a model, a musician passionate about the guitar, and a financial educator through her YouTube channel. Through this platform, she simplifies finance, making it more accessible and actionable for people from diverse backgrounds.
Dr. Nikhil Varma
Dr. Nikhil Varma is an Associate Professor at Ramapo College in New Jersey and Technology Lead for the Algorand Foundation in India. Specializing in Operations and Supply Chain, Blockchain, and Sustainability, he has over 20 years of experience in bridging the gap between technology and business. Nikhil also serves as the Chair of the Blockchain Workgroup at the Center of Excellence at "The Digital Economist," where he leads initiatives at the forefront of blockchain technology and digital economics.
