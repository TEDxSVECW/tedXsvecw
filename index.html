About TEDxSVECW

The Sea of Voices is a celebration of stories, ideas, and connections that shape the world around us. At TEDxSVECW, we believe every voice has the power to inspire change, spark ideas, and build connections. This platform brings together individuals from all walks of life to share their unique experiences, challenge norms, and create ripples of transformation. Life’s uncertainties and challenges often lead to the most profound lessons. These stories—of resilience, risk, and breaking barriers—not only shape who we are but also inspire those around us to dream bigger and aim higher. At TEDxSVECW, we honor the courage it takes to speak out, defy expectations, and make a difference. Like waves in the ocean, each voice adds depth and energy to a greater whole. Through the *Sea of Voices*, we explore the limitless potential of ideas, weaving together diverse perspectives into a powerful narrative of growth, creativity, and connection. Join us in this journey of discovery and transformation, where every story matters, every ripple creates impact, and every voice leaves an echo. Welcome to TEDxSVECW’s *Sea of Voices*—where ideas take flight, connections grow strong, and waves of change begin.Voices, where every wave leaves an indelible mark.

Speakers

Anurag Mittal Co-founder and CEO, Olao Books
Mr. Anurag Mittal is the CEO and Co-founder of Olao Books, a startup aiming to transform accounting and payroll software for small and medium-sized businesses. With leadership roles at Deloitte, JP Morgan, and Icertis, he brings a unique mix of sales, marketing, and operational expertise to the table. Anurag's journey from marketing leader to finance and accounting entrepreneur showcases his versatility and vision. He is dedicated to changing how businesses handle their financial processes, ensuring they have the tools and insights to thrive in a competitive world.
Pooja Vegnesa Content Creator
Ms. Pooja Vegesna is a dedicated mental health counsellor with a passion for promoting slow living and mindfulness. As a social influencer, she shares insights on mental wellness and self-care, encouraging her followers to embrace a more intentional lifestyle. An avid yoga practitioner, Pooja integrates the principles of yoga into her counselling approach, helping individuals find balance and peace. She enjoys reading and sharing knowledge, striving to empower others on their journeys to mental well-being.
Anuvamshi Shettya CEO and Co-founder, AccelRevenue
Ms. Anuvamshi Shetty began her career in investment banking, where she gained valuable insights into the intricacies of finance. However, a pivotal realization soon reshaped her goals, many talented women faced significant obstacles in achieving financial independence. This awareness inspired her to pursue a higher purpose, empowering women across the nation to build businesses, gain financial freedom and overcome societal barriers. Today, she leads a platform dedicated to providing guidance and financial support to female entrepreneurs, helping them forge their own paths to success. Anuvamshi's efforts have garnered recognition, including acknowledgment from MIT Boston's Deshpande Startups, underscoring her belief that even the smallest actions can spark meaningful change.

Dr. Nancy Dhiman

Director & Strategic Implantologist, Harpreet Dental Implant Surgery Centre

Dr. Nancy Dhiman is an internationally certified Strategic Implantologist with extensive experience in Immediate Functional Loading of Implants. Renowned for clinical mastery and surgical precision, with over 20,000 successful bicortical implant placements. A dedicated leader and mentor in advanced implantology, Dr. Dhiman is committed to delivering excellence in patient outcomes, fostering innovation, and advancing the field through teaching and knowledge-sharing. Her training spans prestigious programs in Germany, South Korea, and beyond, focusing on advanced grafting, sinus lift, and prosthetic implant procedures.

Naim Khan

MD, MyFoodMyPrice

Mr. Naim Khan is a visionary entrepreneur and the Founder of MyFoodMyPrice, an innovative e-commerce platform that empowers customers to pre-book meals while helping restaurants optimize operations and significantly reduce food wastage. Through MyFoodMyPrice, he not only addressed these issues but also created a model with profound social impact, reducing wastage, promoting sustainable practices, and making affordable quality food accessible to more people. His journey exemplifies how business innovation can drive meaningful change.
Sri. K. V. Vishnu Raju

Chairman of SVES Society

With a distinguished career spanning multiple industries, this visionary leader has left an indelible mark on the world of education and beyond.
